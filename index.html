Wake up like the Sun.

Your fuel to start a productive day

Based in Manhattan, New York City, we specialize in excellent coffee made with beans sourced from Jamaica. In our local store, we offer a variety of coffee options to enhance your productivity throughout the day. Whether you prefer a rich cup of Jamaican Blue Mountain or a robust espresso creation, we are here to indulge your palate and stimulate your senses.

What makes us special

Through our partnership with the family-owned "Jamaica Gold" farm, we ensure the highest quality and an unparalleled taste profile. Our customers appreciate the complex aromas and the focused energy each cup provides, making every sip a truly exceptional experience. With our coffee, we aim to delight the palate while enhancing focus and productivity.
